Launching the Deaf Ministry

Empowering and Connecting Our Deaf Community

Welcome Message

Dear Brothers and Sisters,

We are excited to announce the launch of our Deaf Ministry at Gitimbine United Methodist Church! Our goal is to create a welcoming, inclusive environment where deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals can experience the love of Christ, grow in faith, and participate fully in our church community.

About the Deaf Ministry

  • Sign language interpretation during services
  • Special Bible study sessions tailored for the deaf
  • Fellowship events and outreach programs
  • Support and counseling for deaf members and visitors

Why a Deaf Ministry?

Because everyone deserves to hear the good news of Jesus Christ. This ministry ensures that deaf and hard-of-hearing persons are embraced as vital members of our church family.
