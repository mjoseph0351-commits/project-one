Our Journey

Gitimbine United Methodist Church began in 2008 with just 30 devoted members, meeting in a modest restaurant behind an office. Though the space was simple, the spirit was strong. In 2017, the church relocated to Kwa Ndhambi, a few meters before Backstreet, marking a new chapter of growth. Today, we are blessed with over 150 members who gather in faith, fellowship, and service.

Activities

Welcome to Gitimbine United Methodist Church, a vibrant and growing congregation rooted in Meru Town, Kenya.

Since our establishment in 2008, we have been dedicated to spreading the love of Christ, nurturing spiritual growth, and serving our community with compassion and faith.

Our Heritage and Affiliation

Gitimbine UMC falls under the Mount Kenya District within the Kenya-Ethiopia Annual Conference of the Methodist Church.

Our church is committed to living out the Christian faith through worship, fellowship, service, and outreach initiatives that transform lives and communities.

Our Ministries

We believe in holistic ministry that addresses spiritual, social, and community needs. To this end, we have established specialized ministries:

Education and Training Programmes

To equip believers for effective service and leadership, we established the UMC Meru School of Theological Studies. This Bible school offers comprehensive theological education and spiritual formation, preparing students for ministry and leadership roles.

In partnership with Breakthrough International Bible University, we currently offer diploma and degree programs in theology. These academic programs aim to deepen biblical understanding and enhance practical ministry skills among our students, fostering a well-equipped church workforce committed to service.

Our Values

We uphold the values of love, integrity, and service as we seek to follow Christ's example.

Vision

To be a church where every member is committed to deepening their faith, actively serving others, and making disciples of all nations, empowered by the Holy Spirit.
